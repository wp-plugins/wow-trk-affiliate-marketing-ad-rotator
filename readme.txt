== Description ==

Start earning money from your website today with the WOW Trk affiliate network! This plugin easily lets you add banner rotators on your site, automatically above or below posts or as a widget on your site. The banner ads automatically rotate, optimise to show the best performing banners and geo target so for example a user in the UK will see a banner for an offer that allows UK traffic.

You can choose the size of the banner rotator from a list of predefined sizes. Ads are from the WOW Trk network so you must be signed up to WOW Trk to use the plugin.

You can register for free here: http://p.wowtrk.com/signup (you will be approved instantly if you verify your phone number!)

WOW Trk has over 250 offers and pays out per conversion. Conversions can be from simply registering at a website for free or make a deposit on a gambling website. Payments can be sent to you via PayPal, Payoneer, Check and Bank Transfer.

Reporting on WOW Trk is instant, so you can easily see how much you are making.

Also easily make even more money with WOW Trk, using other tools like our 'Share Offers' tool which enables you to easily share WOW Trk offers on your social profiles. We also have other creatives for offers including email creatives so you can email your users about offers.

Need help? Simply email support@wowmedia.eu

== Installation ==

1. To use the WOW Trk plugin you must be signed up as an affiliate on WOW Trk. Join at p.wowtrk.com/signup
2. Upload the wowtrk folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Configure the plugin under 'Settings' -> 'WOW Trk'
